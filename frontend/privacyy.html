Privacy Policy

Your privacy and the security of your data are our top priorities.

At Adama Shopping, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Account Details: Your login credentials and user preferences.
  • Transaction Data: Details about payments, orders, and products you buy or sell.
  • Technical Data: Information about your device, browser, and IP address.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data allows us to enhance our service and provide a better, more personalized experience. We use your information to:

  • Process, confirm, and deliver your orders efficiently.
  • Verify user and seller accounts to maintain a trusted marketplace.
  • Communicate important updates, promotional offers, and customer support.
  • Ensure secure transactions and prevent fraudulent activity.

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement robust security measures, including encryption and secure storage systems, to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or misuse. However, please be aware that no online transmission is 100% secure.

4. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, remember your preferences, and collect analytics data to help us improve our platform. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access or request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Update or correct your account information at any time.
  • Request the deletion of your data where it is no longer necessary for us to retain it.
  • Withdraw consent for receiving marketing and promotional messages.
